Adidas 2024 Global Inclusion Week

Theme: Making the Invisible Visible - Neurodiversity

Event Overview

Welcome to Adidas 2024 Global Inclusion Week! This year, our theme is "Making the Invisible Visible: Neurodiversity". We aim to embrace the potential of neurodiversity during this important DEI moment.

Neurodiversity describes the idea that people experience and interact with the world around them in many different ways. There is no "right" way of thinking, learning, and behaving, and differences are not viewed as deficits.

Schedule

Information Dissemination | October 22 (Tuesday)

Creating awareness of the breadth and spread of neurodiversity

Inspiration | October 22-25 (Tuesday to Friday)

Providing a platform to create an inclusive workplace

  • Global Event: Herzo Fireside Chat - My Personal Experience: Living and Working with Neurodiversity
  • GCA Event: GCA Color Wall - Interact with the colorful wall symbolizing the diverse spectrum of neurodiversity experiences
  • GCA Event: DEI Photos - Taken by StudioA

Engagement | October 23-24 (Wednesday to Thursday)

Providing employees with knowledge and resources to accommodate neurodiverse colleagues

  • Global Event: Virtual Workshop - Creating Change
    • Basics and Definitions
    • Social and Workplace Challenges
    • Practical Tips and Action Points
  • China Event: Offline and Online Workshops
    • Education
    • Experience
